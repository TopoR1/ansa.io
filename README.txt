ansa.io is a beautiful, single-player reimagination of agar.io. With its graphics-oriented design, ansa.io seamlessly incorporates hand-crafted images with a spectrum of other features: keyboard control, audio, and artificial intelligence. ansa.io was built utilizing pygame and sklearn.
